Conservation Scientist

  • They work to figure out ways to make sure the environment is safe. They also measure overall health and quality of parks, forests and farmlands and do experiments to make sure that the lands and plants are safe.
  • They basically make sure that the environment is not too affected by other projects we humans do, and how to change the projects if they are negatively affecting the environment.
  • The average salary is $61,000 per year.
  • 18% of conservational scientists have a degree in environmental science. Which is actually a much bigger percentile than you think!
  • 10% also have biology graduates.
