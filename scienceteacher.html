Science Teacher

Physics/math science/political science

  • If you like teaching and want to be one when you grow up, you could be a physics teacher.
  • It is a great science related STEM career where you get to be with kids and teach them.
  • You don't have to follow too many rules like in an office and you can teach kids almost any way you want.
  • The salary for a college physics teacher is $97,650 on average.
  • To be a teacher in a public school you need a license and a degree in the subject.
