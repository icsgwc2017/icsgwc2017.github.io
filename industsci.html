Industrial Scientist

  • An industrial phycologist studies how companies can change the way their workers do work and how to make it best for the workers physiologically when they are working.
  • They figure out ways to make people less stressed during work, they are satisfied with their jobs, the motivation etc.
  • They write about their research and send it too companies that could use it.
  • If it is useful for the companies, they get money.
  • The average salary is $80,000 but highest pay $250,000.
  • You need master's degree in industrial phycology at least, but a better degree means more options.
