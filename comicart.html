Comic book artist

  • If you draw and spend most of your days reading comic books, a job as a comic book artist might be for you!
  • As a comic book artist, you need to have a talent for art, creativity and a certain charismatic.
  • It’s also important to be able to draw human characters accurately.
  • You don’t need to get a degree, but having one is a good idea.
  • The typical salary for a comic book artist is $55,000.
