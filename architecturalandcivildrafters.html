Architectural and Civil Drafters

  • Drafters use computers to make designs for new inventions into detailed blueprints.
  • They make blueprints for computer chips, cars, buildings, and more.
  • They usually work full time on their computers in offices.
  • Work with architects, people who design buildings
  • Help with designing the interior and the structure of buildings.
  • They also can create maps that show the height of the land.
